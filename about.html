About This Site

PassGuard is a free online tool to help you create and evaluate strong passwords. Our goal is to make online security easier for everyone by offering a password generator and a real-time password strength checker.

We do not store or track any passwords. Everything happens in your browser, ensuring your privacy and safety.

Why Strong Passwords?

Weak passwords are the main reason accounts get hacked. By using longer, more complex, and unique passwords for every site, you greatly reduce the risk of data breaches and identity theft.
